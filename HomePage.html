Northwood Shores is a family campground located just north of Rice Lake
Wisconsin, in a tranquil north woods setting. There are 109 campsites
accommodating daily, monthly, and seasonal camping.

We are located on 37 wooded acres with over 3500 feet of unspoiled shoreline of
Upper Devils Lake

Your family will enjoy swimming in the heated pool, fishing form the dock, or just
relaxing on our sandy beach.
